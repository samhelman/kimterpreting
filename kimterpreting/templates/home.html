Services

In-Person Interpreting

I offer interpreting services between British Sign Language (BSL) or Sign Supported English (SSE) and English. This can be in a professional capacity (ie. meetings, conferences) or a personal capacity (ie. health appointments, leisure activities). Basically anywhere that Deaf and hearing people need to communicate with one another.

Video Interpreting

I am also able to offer interpretting services online via "video interpreting". For example video-conferencing and online meetings.

Events

I am available for events. In the past, I have been lucky enough to with organisations such as Sense and at national events including Deaf World, Reading Festival and London Pride. And I would love to work your event too!
